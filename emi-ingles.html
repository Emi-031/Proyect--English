Oscar's Homework Adventure

Story

Oscar asked his mom for permission for his friends, Mónica, Axel, Dylan, Evelyn, and Anna, to do the homework they had left him at home yesterday. His mom said yes, but first he had to wash the dishes and eat the quesadilla she had prepared for him. Oscar, without any problem, complied with the conditions his mom proposed. After that, his friends arrived at 12:00 PM to do their homework, which was to do a report on playing soccer and then do another art assignment based on writing a song.

In the soccer report, Axel and Dylan already had experience, since Axel had played last year and Dylan had been playing for two years. After finishing their homework, their mother prepared dinner, and they all ate happily.
